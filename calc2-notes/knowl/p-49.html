A spring exerts a force of 30 N when stretched 2 m beyond its natural length.

  1. Find the spring constant k then find the force exerted by the spring when stretched 5 meters beyond its natural length.

  2. Find the work that is done by stretching the spring form rest to 5 meters beyond its natural length.

Answer:

a.

\begin{align*} f(x) \amp = k \cdot x\\ f(2) \amp = 30\\ f(2) \amp = k \cdot 2\\ 2k \amp = 30\\ k \amp = 15 \frac{N}{m} \end{align*}
\begin{align*} f(x) \amp = 15x\\ f(5) \amp = 15(5)\\ \amp = 75 N \end{align*}

b.

\begin{align*} W \amp = \int_0^5 15x dx\\ \amp = \left[ \dfrac{15x^2}{2} \right]_0^5\\ \amp = \dfrac{15(5)^2}{2} -\dfrac{15(0)^2}{2}\\ \amp = \dfrac{375}{2}\\ \amp = 187.5 J \end{align*}

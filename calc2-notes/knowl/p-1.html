How can we find the area between two curves?

Note 1.1.2.
Both functions must be continuous on the interval [a,b] and \(f(x) \geq g(x) \) for all \(x\) in [a,b].
For the area between f and g = (area under f) - (area under g), so:

\begin{align*} A \amp = \int_a^b f(x)dx - \int_a^b g(x)dx \\ \amp = \int_a^b f(x)-g(x)dx \end{align*}
